Courses We Offer

Pottery Introduction Course

Course Description

Explore the world of pottery through our "Hand Building Techniques for Pottery" course, designed for creative minds. Delve into hand building, crafting diverse objects with clay using basic tools. Perfect for students, sculptors, homemakers, and anyone seeking a fun artistic experience. Additional lectures on animal sculpture and mask-making enhance artistic skills.

Duration & Pricing

Embark on a one-month journey with our Pottery and Ceramic Introduction Course. Covering both pottery wheel and hand-building techniques in 12 sessions, including six dedicated to hand building and six to wheel throwing. Three sessions per week, each lasting an hour, result in a minimum of five crafted pieces. Priced at ₹8000, this course accommodates all skill levels, providing materials and tools. After six months of practice, advance to flexible, in-depth advanced courses tailored to individual.

Private Pottery Session

Course Description

Immerse in the Artistry: Experience the magic of pottery through our exclusive private sessions. Engage in one-hour clay sessions, where creativity knows no bounds. Craft unique masterpieces with personalized guidance on hand-built techniques, pinching, and personalization. All tools and materials provided for a hassle-free, memorable experience.

Duration & Pricing

Your Pottery Journey Awaits: Explore our private sessions, tailored for individualized experiences. For ₹1000 per person, embark on an hour of artistic expression. Group discounts available at ₹900 each for 4 participants and ₹800 each for groups of 9 or more, fostering shared joy. Flexible scheduling throughout the week, with exclusive time slots and convenient hours. Secure your preferred time with a ₹1000 confirmation charge. Join us for an enriching hour, bringing your artistic vision to life.
