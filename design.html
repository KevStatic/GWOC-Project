Frequently Asked Questions

Why learn pottery?
Learning pottery provides a creative outlet, and offers a therapeutic and meditative experience.
Who is the introductory course suitable for?
The course caters to individuals of all skill levels, making it suitable for those exploring pottery for the first time or seeking to deepen their understanding of clay as a sculptural material.
What opportunities do advanced pottery courses provide?
Advanced courses offer in-depth exploration of various techniques to achieve the desired level of expertise.
How long should one practice before progressing to advanced courses?
Participants are recommended to practice for about six months before considering advanced courses.
What is the structure of a private pottery session?
Private sessions offer a personalized, one-hour clay experience covering hand-built techniques, pinching, and personalization, with all materials provided.
