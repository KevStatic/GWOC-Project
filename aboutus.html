About Mudberry Studio

Jaydeep Kotadiya

Jaydeep Kotadiya is an accomplished Ceramic Art teacher at Surat College of Fine Art, VNSGU, holding a Master of Fine Arts (M.F.A.) in Ceramic & Glass from Vishwa Bharti University.With a bachelor's degree in sculpture, he completed his master's in Ceramic from Santiniketan in 2018. Since 2019, he has been a Teaching Assistant at Surat College of Fine Art, sharing his knowledge and skills. Alongside his academic
role, he owns Mudberry Pottery Studio, where he combines teaching and hands-on artistry, fostering creativity in the community.Mr. Kotadiya has showcased his expertise in art exhibitions and actively participated in workshops, continuing to inspire in the field of ceramic art.
